About the App

Protein Sequence Viewer is an iOS app that allows you to quickly retrieve protein sequences (1-letter code) and lengths (aa) from UniProtKB/Swiss-Prot (reviewed) using gene names or UniProt IDs (Accession/Entry name).

The app primarily displays canonical sequences, with the ability to switch to isoforms via dropdown menu when available.

Additionally, you can download databases for Human, Mouse, Rat, and Yeast to your device for offline search and browsing.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1. Multiple candidates appear when searching by gene name
A. Multiple entries may exist for the same gene. Please select the desired entry from the candidate list (only reviewed entries are shown).
Q2. Isoforms are not displayed
A. If the target entry does not have isoform information, the switch menu will not be displayed.
Q3. Unable to search offline
A. Please verify that you have downloaded the offline data for the target species. If the download is incomplete, please try again from the settings screen.
Q4. "No results" is displayed
A. Please check the input spelling, species selection, and whether the target exists in Swiss-Prot (reviewed).
