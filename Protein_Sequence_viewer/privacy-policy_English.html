This Privacy Policy sets forth how user information is handled in this app. By using this app, users are deemed to have agreed to this Policy.

This app does not require account registration or login, and the operator does not directly collect personal information such as names, addresses, or phone numbers in principle.

However, for the purposes of ad delivery and stable operation, the following information may be collected by third-party services (Google AdMob/Google Mobile Ads SDK).

Google Mobile Ads SDK may collect the following information for ad delivery, fraud prevention, and performance improvement:

※The above may vary depending on SDK specifications and settings.

2.2 ATT (App Tracking Transparency) Information

On iOS, tracking-related data (e.g., IDFA) may be used for purposes such as ad optimization only with user permission. The reason for requesting permission is displayed through iOS mechanisms.

When users contact us with inquiries, we may collect email addresses, inquiry content, and device information (model/OS version, etc.) to the extent necessary for responding.

3. Purpose of Information Use

Collected information is used for the following purposes:

This app uses Google services (AdMob/Google Mobile Ads SDK) for ad delivery. Accordingly, information is handled in accordance with Google's privacy policies.

Additionally, this app communicates with the UniProt API to retrieve UniProt data (search terms are included in communications).
